pochita saw:
(light gray yarn)
R1: mr 6sc (6 stitches total)
R2: (2sc, inc) x2 (8 stitches total)
R3-R5: 8sc (8 stitches total)
squish the saw flat
sc the saw shut
exchange light gray yarn for dark gray yarn
(make sure the light gray yarn's working yarn is long, and sew in the light gray yarn's tail yarn)
(make sure both the light gray yarn's working yarn and tail yarn are long)
(2ch, slst) around
pochita body (until face):
(orange yarn)
R1: mr 6sc (6 stitches total)
R2: 6inc (12 stitches total)
R3: (sc, inc) x 6 (18 stitches total)
R4: (2sc, inc) x 6 (24 stitches total)
R5: (3sc, inc) x 6 (30 stitches total)
R6-R8: 30sc (30 stitches total)
pochita face (eyes):
(white yarn)
place safety eyes between rounds 4-5
(with a needle) wrap yarn around the safety eyes twice
adjust eye size with needle
sew in the yarn ends with the inside of the pochita face
pochita face (saw):
pass through dark gray yarn's working yarn into the middle
pass through dark gray yarn's tail yarn and the light gray yarn's working yarn into the top of the head (vertically)
tie the dark gray yarn's working yarn with the dark gray yarn's tail yarn
whipstitch the light gray yarn's working yarn with the flat part of the saw
go from top to center then center to top with the whipstitching
sew in the light gray yarn's working yarn with the inside of the pochita face
pochita face (smile):
make a :3 smile
make sure to do it symmetrically
the smile can also be set up like u-u
pochita body (to tail / end):
(still orange yarn)
R9-R14: 30sc (30 stitches total)
R15: (3sc, dec) x 6 (24 stitches total)
R16: (2sc, dec) x 6 (18 stitches total)
R17: (sc, dec) x 6 (12 stitches total)
R18: 3dec, 2inc, 2dec (9 stitches total)
stuff
R19: 9sc (9 stitches total)
R20: 4dec (5 stitches total) then dec
sew yarn, tie yarn, and shove yarn into pochita
pochita short leg:
(orange yarn)
R1: mr 6sc (6 stitches total)
R2-R3: 6sc (6 stitches total)
squish the leg flat
close the leg with sc
make 2
pochita long leg:
(orange yarn)
R1: mr 6sc (6 stitches total)
R2-R4: 6sc (6 stitches total)
squish the leg flat
close the leg with sc
make 2
pochita legs:
sew however you would like (just make sure the way you sew is the same for each leg)
(imagine legs take up 3 stitches of width)
shle = short leg
lole = long leg

pochita lower handle:
(black yarn)
do this below the tail
3ch
slst into the 2nd ch
4ch
slst into the 2nd ch
2ch
slst into pochita's body to secure
use the working and tail yarn to make a double knot
stuff the double knot into pochita's body
pochita ripcord:
(black yarn)
do this at the end of pochita's tail
8ch
slst into the 2nd ch
4ch
slst into the 2nd ch
2ch
slst into the 3rd ch, 2nd ch, and 1st ch of the original 8ch
use the working and tail yarn to make a double knot
stuff the double knot into pochita's body
pochita upper handle:
(black yarn)
this should be done two rounds ahead of where the saw comes out of the top of pochita's head
the space between the two vertical parts of the upper handle should be ~5-6 stitches
3ch
slst into the 2nd ch
8 ch
slst into the 2nd ch
2ch
slst into pochita's body to secure
use the working and tail yarn to make a double knot
stuff the double knot into pochita's body
pochita key chain:
nothing special (just use the same technique as most amigurumi keychains would use)
